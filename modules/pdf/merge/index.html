Secure, Browser-Native PDF Merging

Toolix's PDF Merge utility allows you to combine multiple PDF files into one ordered document directly inside your web browser. Built on local Javascript execution libraries, this tool is 100% browser-native. This means your files are parsed and assembled entirely on your client machine and never uploaded to any server.

Why use Toolix PDF Merger?

  • Complete Privacy: Your sensitive files never travel over the network. Ideal for corporate documents, invoices, and personal IDs.
  • No Upload Limits: Drag and drop gigabytes of files. Since processing is local, you are not constrained by server upload limits.
  • Drag-and-Drop Reordering: Easily sort cards horizontally to match your target merging sequence.

Who is it for?

Ideal for legal practitioners compiling litigation bundles, researchers stitching papers, students assembling assignments, and remote workers combining project receipts.
