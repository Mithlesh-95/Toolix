Browser-Native PDF Splitting

Toolix's PDF Split utility enables you to divide your PDF document into individual pages or custom ranges without uploading files to third-party servers. All operations happen in JavaScript on your local machine, keeping private corporate files, scan logs, and statements secure.

Why use Toolix PDF Splitter?

  • Privacy First: Completely local compilation. Your files stay strictly in the sandbox of your browser.
  • Flexible Ranges: Extract specific sections (e.g. 1-3, 5, 8-10) or split every single page into single-page PDF files download-ready in a single ZIP.
  • Zero Server Load: Process large documents instantly.

Who is it for?

Excellent for administrators selecting pages of scanned contracts, accounting departments splitting monthly invoices, and students extracting specific workbook segments.
